Introduction

Welcome to Verify's Privacy Policy. This document outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our platform. At Verify, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data.

This Privacy Policy applies to all services offered by Verify, including our website, mobile applications, and verification services. By using our platform, you consent to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

Information Collection

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services. The information we collect depends on how you interact with our platform and which services you use.

Personal Information

  • Full name, contact details (email, phone)
  • Identity documents (ID number, passport)
  • Employment history and qualifications
  • References and work experience
  • Background check data (Silver/Gold tiers)
  • Polygraph results (Gold tier)

Account Information

  • Login credentials and preferences
  • Subscription and payment details
  • Verification tier/status

Usage Information

  • Device info (IP, browser, OS)
  • Log data and activity
  • Location data (if enabled)

Data Usage

We use collected information for:

Service Provision

  • User account management
  • Identity & qualification verification
  • Connecting job seekers & employers
  • Payment processing

Communication

  • Service updates & notifications
  • Support responses
  • Feature announcements

Platform Improvement

  • Usage analysis
  • UX enhancements
  • Feature development

Legal Compliance

  • Fraud prevention
  • Regulatory compliance
  • Terms enforcement

Security Measures

We implement robust measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: Data encrypted in transit & at rest.
  • Access Controls: Strict internal permissions.
  • Audits: Regular security assessments.
  • Training: Staff trained on data protection.
  • Secure Infrastructure: Hardened data centers.

No system is 100% secure, but we strive to protect your information.

User Rights

You have rights over your data:

Access & Portability

Request a copy of your personal data.

Correction

Update inaccurate information.

Deletion

Ask us to delete your data in certain cases.

Restrict & Object

Request processing restrictions or object to it.

To exercise these rights, contact our Privacy Team. We respond within 30 days.

Cookies Policy

We use cookies to enhance your experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device.

  • Essential Cookies:

    Necessary for site functionality.

  • Preference Cookies:

    Remember your settings.

  • Analytics Cookies:

    Understand site usage.

  • Marketing Cookies:

    Deliver relevant ads.

You can manage cookies via your browser. Blocking essential cookies may break features.

Third-Party Cookies

Third parties (analytics, social media) may set cookies. Refer to their policies for details.

Third-Party Sharing

We share data only in these situations:

Service Providers

Partners who assist us (e.g. hosting, payment). They must protect your data.

Employers

Verified details shared with employers who purchase access.

Verification Partners

Third-party services for background checks and polygraphs.

Legal Requirements

When required by law or to protect rights and safety.

We do not sell your data. Third parties must uphold our privacy standards.
